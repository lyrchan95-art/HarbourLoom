About Us

Discover the story behind our passion for craftsmanship and community

Anna and I first visited Dhaka on a mission trip, where we were deeply moved by the resilience, creativity, and warmth of the people we met. That experience inspired us to create this social enterprise in Hong Kong — a way to give back to the society that welcomed us and to share the beauty of Bangladeshi craftsmanship with the world.

Aarong

Empowering artisans through fair trade and heritage preservation

Aarong, founded by BRAC, empowers over 65,000 artisans across Bangladesh. Their mission is to preserve heritage crafts while ensuring fair wages and sustainable livelihoods. Each product carries a story of skill, tradition, and hope.

Bangladesh

A land of vibrant culture and rich textile traditions

Bangladesh is a land of rivers, vibrant culture, and rich traditions. Known for its textiles, handloom weaving, and intricate crafts, the country is also home to resilient communities who continue to thrive despite challenges.
